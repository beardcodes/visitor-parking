Visitor Parking Terms and Conditions

Four Seasons Private Residences Kuala Lumpur

1. General Provisions

These terms and conditions govern the use of the visitor parking facilities at Four Seasons Private Residences.

By utilizing the visitor parking, all visitors agree to comply with these terms.

2. Eligibility

Visitor parking is available for guests of residents only. Residents must inform their guests of these terms.

Each resident is allotted a specific number of visitor parking permits, which must be displayed in the vehicle.

3. Duration of Parking

The visitors shall not park at the visitor carpark bays for more than twenty-four (24) hours.

The transponder (parking access card) has a validity period of 24 hours from the time it is first received. After this 24-hour period, the transponder will automatically become inactive, and the exit barrier gate will not open without assistance from the management office.

4. Responsibilities

Visitors are responsible for ensuring their vehicle is parked within designated parking spaces.

The building is not liable for any damage, theft, or loss of property that occurs while the vehicle is parked on the premises.

5. Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited:

  • Parking in spaces designated for residents or other unauthorized areas.
  • Abandoning vehicles or leaving them unattended for extended periods.
  • Using the visitor parking for commercial purposes or over 24 hours stays.

6. Enforcement

The management reserves the right to wheel clamp any vehicles that violate these terms and conditions without notice, at the owner's expense.

Repeated violations may result in the revocation of visitor parking privileges.

7. Amendments

The management reserves the right to amend these terms and conditions at any time. Visitors will be notified of any changes.

8. Contact Information

For any questions or concerns regarding visitor parking, please contact the building management at 03-2856 7959 or concierge desk at 03-2382 8855.
